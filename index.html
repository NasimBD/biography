My story

I would like to tell a short story about myself. A few years ago I was a physics PhD student and despite the fact that I was the top student and my supervisor was very content with the progress of my thesis, I decided to think long and hard about who I am and what my interests are. I took a trip to a peaceful coastal city and eventually gathered my courage to give up PhD. Afterwards, I commenced learning web design and today, it is one of the greatest pleasures of my life...

Besides coding, I love TED talks, reading and exercising. Also, I belong to a startup team whose goal is to protect our beautiful planet Earth.
